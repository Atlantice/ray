Project RayFugee

rays me up

  • Develop a peer-to-peer e-commerce marketplace

  • Platform will facilitate the entrepreneurial visions of refugees who are seeking to build upon artisans skills brought from their homeland.

  • RayMakers (Refugee artisans/craftsman) can sell their unique handmade item to the global community.

Hult Prize Competition

Reawaken human potential

  • We are pursuing the Hult Prize which will provide us with a seed grant in order to help us create our idea into a sustainable social enterprise.

  • The Hult Prize serves is to bring forward entrepreneurs who will address the most pressing social issues faced across the world through the development of sustainable social enterprises.

  • This year, the task is to restore the rights and dignity to the tens of millions of refugees who have been displaced due to economic hardships and war-torn homelands.
